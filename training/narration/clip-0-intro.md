# Clip 0 — "Welcome to the TPAR app" (~70 seconds)

The series opener: what the app IS before any page gets explained. Beat 1 plays over a
branded title card — deliberately built as a swappable front segment so a host (Danny
selfie-video, or a synthetic avatar) can replace the card later without re-recording the tour.
Voice: same warm narrator as clip 1. Beats sync to the recorder's beat manifest, not fixed times.

**Beat 1 — title card (host slot)**
> Welcome to the TPAR app. This is your toolbox for everything that isn't a wrench — built by us, around how we actually work. Give me one minute and you'll have the whole map. The short clips after this one go deep on each piece.

**Beat 2 — My Day**
> Everything starts on My Day — your home base. Your clock, the day's expectations, the team whiteboard, your quick actions, today's appointments, and your daily wrap. One page, everything in reach.

**Beat 3 — Jobs / Find**
> Every job and every customer we've ever touched lives in here. Search by name, by address — or just describe what you remember.

**Beat 4 — Estimate builder + Price it with me**
> Estimates get built here — options for the customer, never pressure. And when you don't know what to charge, describe the work in your own words: it asks you the judgment questions, then builds the line items with you — priced the TPAR way. Time, material, and your judgment.

**Beat 5 — Ask**
> Stuck on anything — pricing, process, where something lives? Ask. It knows how we work and it knows our data. Thirty seconds, standing in the yard.

**Beat 6 — Field Guide + close**
> And the Field Guide holds how we carry ourselves — the money ladder, the principles, and exactly who to call when you're stuck. That's the map. Next clip: My Day, up close.
